Our Theory of Change

We seek to create a global network of females in STEM that will provide all females in the field with a united front. This network, decentralised at its roots and inclusive of all females from as young as six years, provides academic and career counselling, mentorship, networking events, recommendation & provision of internships, training and job opportunities in STEM.
